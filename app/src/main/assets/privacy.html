PRIVACY POLICY

This Privacy Policy ("Policy") outlines how GPSSample Mobile App ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and safeguards data obtained through the GPSSample mobile application ("GPSSample"). By using GPSSample, you agree to the practices described in this Policy.

  1. User-Provided Data: GPSSample allows the user to collect data including text, images, or other information for users' survey purposes. This data is not provided or accessed by GPSSample development team.
  2. Location Information: GPSSample may request access to the device's location to enable location-based data collection. This information is only shared and maintained by the users, and user designated cloud storage. Location data is not stored within the app, or on user devices. 
  3. How We Use Your Data: We will not use or access your data.
  4. Security: We employ best practices for data security to protect data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. GPSSample uses 128-bit Advanced Encryption Standards (AES) for data encryption in transit. While we strive to ensure the security of data, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.
  5. Data Ownership: Users (e.g., survey or study managers) retain ownership of the data they provide through GPSSample. We do not sell, rent, lease or otherwise share user data to third parties.
  6. Third-Party Services: GPSSample may integrate with third-party services or contain links to external websites. This Privacy Policy does not cover the practices of third parties, and users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of those services. Information about third-party services, including Mapbox and ODK, are included in the Technical documentation.
  7. Changes to this Policy: We may update this Policy to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. Users will be notified of significant changes, and the updated Policy will be effective upon posting.
  8. Contact Information: If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, or GPSSample, you may contact us at gpssample23@gmail.com. Additional technical documentation is available on the GPSSample GitHub repository.

 

By using GPSSample, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.
